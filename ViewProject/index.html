The peregrination of the marmalade was a peculiar sight to behold. It had started its journey atop a stack of pancakes, slowly oozing its way down the sides until it reached the plate. From there, it was scooped up and spread onto a slice of toast, where it continued its meandering path. The marmalade seemed to have a mind of its own, dodging butter and crumbs as it made its way across the bread. Finally, it came to rest at the very edge of the toast, as if contemplating its next move. It was a strange thing to witness, but somehow mesmerizing all the same.
